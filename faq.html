FAQ

How do I navigate the site?

From the home page, you can utilize the tabs to navigate to the different parts of the website. Statistics has information regarding schools, horses, and riders. Shows has upcoming show listings, registration for shows, and seat reservation. Messages has a messaging system that allows you to message users from Gallop.

How do I message someone?

In the messaging tab, enter your email address, email's password, and the email you wish to message. The receiver will then be notified of your messaged from Gallop.

How do I reserve a seat at a show?

After navigating to the Upcoming Shows tab, select the show you wish to attend. Click the "Reserve Seat" button corresponding to the show. You will then be directed to a page with the show's information, including available seats. Click the "Reserve Seat" button once more and you will receive a registration code to provide on arrival to the event. Please note that if the show is full, you will be unable to reserve a seat.
